Famous Places of Varanasi

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this temple is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas and holds immense religious significance. It is a spiritual epicenter for millions of devotees visiting Varanasi.

Dashashwamedh Ghat

One of the most vibrant and ancient ghats in Varanasi, Dashashwamedh Ghat is known for its elaborate Ganga Aarti ceremony held every evening that draws crowds from around the world.

Sarnath

Located just outside Varanasi, Sarnath is where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon after attaining enlightenment. The site features stupas, museums, and a tranquil atmosphere.

Ramnagar Fort

Built in the 18th century, Ramnagar Fort stands across the Ganga river. The fort houses a museum showcasing royal artifacts, vintage cars, and is still home to the royal family of Varanasi.
