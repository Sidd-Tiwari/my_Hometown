Festivals of Varanasi

Dev Deepawali

Celebrated fifteen days after Diwali, Dev Deepawali lights up the ghats of Varanasi with thousands of earthen lamps, creating a surreal, divine atmosphere to welcome the gods.

Mahashivratri

One of the most revered festivals dedicated to Lord Shiva, Mahashivratri is marked by fasting, night-long worship, and special prayers at the Kashi Vishwanath Temple.

Ganga Mahotsav

A five-day cultural celebration held along the ghats of the Ganga, this festival features music, dance, crafts, and culminates in Dev Deepawali — showcasing Varanasi’s spiritual and artistic richness.
